System Overview & Key Links
What was built and how everything connects

The GWM Daily Sales Reporting Tool replaces the previous Excel-based weekly process with a fast, mobile-first web application. Dealers submit their daily figures in under 60 seconds. Data flows automatically into a central Google Sheet, which feeds the live dashboard and Power BI.

What dealers use

A simple mobile web form — no app download required. Works on any phone or desktop browser.

What GWM staff use

The internal dashboard shows live compliance, KPIs, model-level activity, and direction split.

Where data lives

Every submission is stored as flat rows in Google Sheets — one row per model bucket per dealer per day.

How Power BI connects

Power BI reads directly from Google Sheets via a live CSV URL. No manual exports needed.

Dealer Guide
How to submit your daily sales report — takes under 60 seconds
📱 Mobile first. This form is designed for phones. Bookmark the link on your home screen for fastest daily access.

How to Submit

  • 1
    Open https://gwm-anz.github.io/gwm-sales-reporting/ on your phone or computer. Bookmark it for daily use.
  • 2
    Select your Dealer from the dropdown. Your selection is remembered for next time.
  • 3
    Enter your name in the "Submitted By" field. This is remembered between sessions.
  • 4
    Select Direction vs Yesterday — Up, Flat, or Down. This reflects overall traffic/activity sentiment for the day.
  • 5
    Fill in the Model Bucket Grid. Enter numbers for each model you have activity on. Models with no activity can be left as 0. Use Tab to move between cells quickly.
  • 6
    Check the Live Totals bar at the top of the grid — it shows your running total for Enquiries, Test Drives, New Sold, Fleet, Demo Sold, and Forecast.
  • 7
    Press Submit Daily Report. You'll see a confirmation screen. Done.
⏰ Submit before 10:00 AM. Reports submitted after 10:00 AM are automatically flagged as late. A red "LATE" badge appears in the header as a reminder.

Model Bucket Grid — What to Enter

ColumnWhat to enter
EnquiryTotal customer enquiries received today for this model (walk-in, phone, online)
Test DrivesNumber of test drives conducted today for this model
New SoldNumber of new vehicle sales contracted today
Fleet (5+)Fleet deals of 5 or more units — enter total units, not number of deals
Demo SoldNumber of demonstrator vehicles sold today
ForecastYour forecast for total deliveries/handovers this month for this model
Tip: You only need to fill in models where you have activity. Daily activity fields start blank so a true zero can be separated from a missed field. Enter 0 where there was no activity; forecast carries through the month unless changed.

Bookmarking to Your Home Screen

iPhone (Safari)

Open the link in Safari → tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add. The form icon will appear on your home screen.

Android (Chrome)

Open the link in Chrome → tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" → tap Add. Works like an app shortcut.

Funnel KPI Cards

The added funnel cards show how effectively daily activity is moving through the sales pipeline.

Funnel KPIWhat it means
Enq → TD %Test Drives ÷ Enquiries. Shows how well enquiry activity converts into showroom or drive activity.
TD → Sold %Sold including demos ÷ Test Drives. Shows closing strength after customer engagement.
Enq → Sold %Sold including demos ÷ Enquiries. Shows full funnel conversion from enquiry to sale.
Sold Inc DemoNew Sold + Demo Sold. Shows total sales activity captured for the day.
Demo Mix %Demo Sold ÷ Sold including demos. Shows reliance on demonstrator sales versus normal new vehicle sales.
Colour indicators:
Green = strong performance
Red = weak performance
Neutral = within normal range
How to interpret the funnel:
Low Enq → TD % can indicate an engagement or showroom conversion issue.
Low TD → Sold % can indicate a closing, pricing, stock, or product-fit issue.
High Demo Mix % can indicate reliance on demo activity rather than normal new vehicle sales.

Filters

Use the filter bar to narrow data by Date, Dealer, Region, or Model. Click Apply to refresh all KPIs, charts, and tables.

Tabs

Dealer Compliance

Full list of all 30 dealers with submission status, time, late flag, and summary figures.

Model Summary

All 19 model buckets with aggregate totals and conversion rate (New Sold ÷ Enquiries).

Row Detail

Individual submission rows with all figures. Shows up to 200 rows — export CSV for full data.

Charts

Direction split donut (Up/Flat/Down) and daily enquiry trend line for 7, 14, or 30 days.

Refreshing Data

Click the ↻ Refresh button in the header to pull the latest submissions. The dashboard does not auto-refresh — click manually when you want the latest view.

Exporting CSV

Click ⬇ Export CSV in the filter bar to download all currently filtered rows as a CSV file, matching the Google Sheet schema exactly.

Power BI — Connecting & Building Reports
Live connection from Google Sheets to Power BI Desktop
Prerequisite: The Google Sheet must be set to "Anyone with the link can view" (see Section 4 above) before Power BI can connect.

Step 1 — Connect Power BI to the Data

  • 1
    Open Power BI Desktop → click Get Data → select Web
  • 2
    Paste this URL and click OK:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1RgHHcIgq_onYKgh9PC6IqsFXxTWbgM_GAVxJSV0NPrY/gviz/tq?tqx=out:csv&sheet=submissions
  • 3
    In the preview, click Transform Data to open Power Query
  • 4
    Click "Use First Row as Headers" if not already applied
  • 5
    Set column data types (see table below)
  • 6
    Click Close & Apply

Step 3 — Key DAX Measures to Create

In Power BI, click New Measure and add these:

-- Compliance rate (30 = total dealers in network)
Compliance % =
DIVIDE(
    DISTINCTCOUNT(submissions[dealer_code]),
    30
) * 100

-- Enquiry to sale conversion
Conversion Rate % =
DIVIDE(
    SUM(submissions[new_sold]),
    SUM(submissions[enquiry])
) * 100

-- Late submission count
Late Submissions =
CALCULATE(
    DISTINCTCOUNT(submissions[dealer_code]),
    submissions[is_late] = TRUE()
)

-- Dealers who submitted today
Dealers Submitted =
DISTINCTCOUNT(submissions[dealer_code])

-- Dealers missing (total - submitted)
Dealers Missing = 30 - [Dealers Submitted]

-- Total new sold (use for KPI card)
Total New Sold = SUM(submissions[new_sold])

-- Total enquiries
Total Enquiries = SUM(submissions[enquiry])

Step 5 — Set Up Automatic Refresh

  • 1
    Publish your report to Power BI Service (powerbi.com)
  • 2
    In Power BI Service → go to your dataset → Settings → Scheduled Refresh
  • 3
    Enable refresh → set frequency to Daily at e.g. 11:00 AM AEST (after all dealers should have submitted)
  • 4
    Add your email for refresh failure notifications
Free tier limit: Power BI Pro allows up to 8 scheduled refreshes per day. The free tier allows 1 per day. For near-real-time data, consider using Power BI Premium or manually refreshing the dataset.

Troubleshooting & FAQs
Common questions and how to fix them

Form Issues

ProblemSolution
Form won't submit — "required fields" errorMake sure Dealer, Submitted By, and Direction are all filled in before submitting
Shows "LATE" badge in headerIt's past 10:00 AM — your report will be flagged late but will still submit successfully
Page won't load on phoneCheck your internet connection. Try opening in Chrome or Safari. The site is at gwm-anz.github.io/gwm-sales-reporting/
Submitted but nothing in Google SheetWait 30 seconds and refresh the sheet. If still empty, contact your GWM rep — the backend may need reconnecting
Dealer name not in dropdownContact GWM-ANZ to have the dealer added to the system

Dashboard Issues

ProblemSolution
Dashboard showing demo data (orange banner)Click the ⚙ button (bottom right) and paste the Apps Script URL
Dealer shows as "Missing" but they submittedCheck the date filter — ensure it's set to today's date. Click Apply.
Charts not loadingRefresh the page. Charts require an internet connection to load Chart.js
Export CSV is emptyMake sure your filters return data — try clearing all filters and exporting again

Power BI Issues

ProblemSolution
Power BI can't connect to the URLEnsure the Google Sheet is shared as "Anyone with the link can view"
Data not refreshing in Power BI ServiceWeb connector data sources use anonymous credentials — check the gateway settings aren't blocking anonymous access
Headers showing as Column1, Column2In Power Query, click "Use First Row as Headers"
Date column showing as textIn Power Query, click the column header → Change Type → Date
